# COMPLETE ADVANTAGES LIST v4.0

---

## ADVANTAGES PHILOSOPHY

Advantages are consolidated into **base types** with **modifiers** that adjust cost. This creates a flexible system where you can customize powers to fit your concept.

**Format:** Advantage Name [Base CP Cost]
- Description
- **Modifiers:** List of common modifiers with CP adjustments

---

## PHYSICAL ENHANCEMENT ADVANTAGES

### ENHANCED ATTRIBUTE [10 CP per +1]
Increase one of your core attributes: STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, or CHA.
- Each +1 costs 10 CP
- Maximum +10 without GM permission
- Stacks with racial/class bonuses
- **Examples:** Enhanced STR +3 [30 CP], Enhanced INT +5 [50 CP]

---

### DAMAGE RESISTANCE (DR) [5 CP per point]
Reduce all incoming damage by your DR value.
- Applies after armor AC calculation
- Stacks with other sources of DR
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Specific Type Only (-40%):** Only vs physical, only vs energy, only vs fire, etc.
  - **Hardened (+20%):** Cannot be bypassed by armor-piercing
  - **Force Field (+30%):** Can be turned on/off, projects outward visibly
  - **Ablative (-10%):** DR decreases permanently when damaged until repaired
- **Examples:** 
  - DR 10 [50 CP] - Superman-lite
  - DR 5 (Physical only) [15 CP] - Tough skin
  - DR 20 (Force Field) [130 CP] - Energy shield

---

### REGENERATION [Variable]
Heal HP over time automatically.
- **Base Costs:**
  - **Slow (1 HP/hour)** [10 CP]
  - **Regular (1 HP/10 minutes)** [25 CP]
  - **Fast (1 HP/minute)** [40 CP]
  - **Very Fast (1 HP/round)** [65 CP]
  - **Extreme (10 HP/round)** [100 CP]
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Fatigue Only (-50%):** Only heals FP, not HP
  - **Regrowth (+50%):** Can regrow lost limbs and organs
  - **Unkillable (+100%):** Can regenerate from a single cell/piece
  - **Requires Rest (-20%):** Only heals while resting
  - **Requires Specific Condition (-30%):** Only in sunlight, only when touching earth, etc.
  - **Limited Use (-20%):** Only X times per day
- **Examples:** 
  - Wolverine: Very Fast + Regrowth [105 CP]
  - Deadpool: Extreme + Unkillable [200 CP]
  - Trolls: Fast + Requires Darkness [32 CP]

### ALTERED PHYSIOLOGY [Variable, base 30-50 CP]
Your body has fundamentally non-human properties.

**LIQUID FORM [35 CP]**
- Body is liquid (water, mercury, etc.)
- Immune to critical hits and piercing damage
- Resistance to bludgeoning damage
- Can flow through any gap
- Vulnerable to cold (frozen) and fire (evaporated)
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Combat Form (+15 CP):** Can form solid weapons from body
  - **Absorb Liquids (+10 CP):** Grow by absorbing water/similar substances
  - **Specific Liquid (-10%):** Only water, only oil, etc.

**GASEOUS FORM [40 CP]**
- Body is gas or smoke
- Immune to physical damage
- Cannot be grappled
- Fly 40 ft at will
- Vulnerable to wind and containment
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Toxic Cloud (+20 CP):** Enemies in your space take poison damage
  - **Dispersal (+10 CP):** Can spread over large area

**SWARM FORM [40 CP]**
- Body composed of many small units (insects, bees, nanobots)
- Immune to single-target attacks
- Vulnerable to area attacks (×2 damage)
- Can split and fit through tiny spaces
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Hive Mind (+10 CP):** Units share consciousness perfectly
  - **Reconstitute (+20 CP):** Can reform even if mostly destroyed
  - **Specific Type (-0%):** Specify what you're made of (bees, rats, etc.)

**CONSTRUCT [50 CP]**
- Body is artificial (robot, android, golem)
- Don't need to eat, drink, breathe, or sleep
- Immune to poison and disease
- Immune to aging
- Cannot be healed by normal medicine/magic (must be repaired)
- Vulnerable to construct-specific effects (rust, EMP, etc.)
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Superior Construction (+30 CP):** Gain DR 5 and resistance to one damage type
  - **Living Construct (+10 CP):** Can be healed by magic
  - **Obvious (-5%):** Clearly artificial (metal skin, glowing eyes)

**PLANT BODY [35 CP]**
- Body is living wood/plant matter
- Regenerate when touching earth (1 HP/round)
- Can photosynthesize (don't need food if in sunlight)
- Vulnerable to fire (×1.5 damage)
- Vulnerable to blight/herbicides
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Wooden Hide (+10 CP):** Natural DR 5
  - **Thorns (+10 CP):** Melee attackers take 1d4 piercing

**ELEMENTAL BODY [45 CP]**
- Body is made of pure element (fire, ice, stone, lightning, etc.)
- Choose one element
- Immune to your element's damage
- Vulnerable to opposing element (×2 damage)
- Don't need to eat or breathe
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Living Element (+20 CP):** Can control ambient element around you
  - **Elemental Aura (+15 CP):** Enemies in melee range take 1d6 elemental damage

**Examples:**
- Hydro-Man: Liquid Form + Absorb Liquids [45 CP]
- Swarm: Swarm Form (bees) + Hive Mind [50 CP]
- Vision: Construct + Superior Construction [80 CP]
- Groot: Plant Body + Wooden Hide [45 CP]

### STRIKING STRENGTH [15 CP]
Your unarmed attacks are exceptionally powerful.
- Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 base damage instead of 1d4
- Scales with character level: 1d8 at 5th level, 1d10 at 11th, 1d12 at 17th
- Can be combined with Natural Weapons
- **Examples:** Martial artists, brawlers, super-strong characters

---

### NATURAL WEAPONS [Variable]
You have built-in weapons (claws, fangs, horns, tail, etc.).
- **Base Costs by Damage:**
  - **Light (1d4)** [8 CP]
  - **Medium (1d6)** [10 CP]
  - **Heavy (1d8)** [15 CP]
  - **Massive (1d10)** [20 CP]
  - **Giant (1d12)** [25 CP]
- Damage scales with STR as normal
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Adamantium/Unbreakable (+20 CP):** Cannot be destroyed, bypass DR
  - **Retractable (+5 CP):** Can hide weapons at will
  - **Poisoned (+15 CP):** Add poison damage or save-or effect
  - **Energy-Based (+10 CP):** Count as magical weapons, bypass resistance
  - **Two Weapons (+50%):** Have paired weapons (two claws), can dual-wield
  - **Reach (+10 CP):** 10 ft reach instead of 5 ft
- **Examples:** 
  - Wolverine: 1d8 claws, Adamantium, Retractable [40 CP]
  - Sabretooth: 1d6 claws, Two Weapons [15 CP]
  - Dragon: 2d10 bite, Reach [50 CP]

## DEFENSIVE ADVANTAGES

### FORCE FIELD [35 CP base]
Create protective energy barriers.
- Barrier has 20 HP, AC 15
- Lasts 1 minute
- Protects 5 ft radius (self + 1 adjacent ally)
- 3 uses per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Large Area (+15 CP):** 20 ft radius
  - **Dome Shape (+10 CP):** Protects from all directions
  - **Selective Permeability (+10 CP):** Allies can shoot out, enemies can't shoot in
  - **Reflective (+20 CP):** Reflects ranged attacks back at attacker
  - **Always On (+25 CP):** Permanent passive shield, regenerates HP
  - **Ablative (-10%):** HP decreases permanently until repaired
  - **Personal Only (-20%):** Only protects self
- **Examples:**
  - Invisible Woman: Dome + Selective + Large Area [70 CP]
  - Violet Parr: Personal + Always On [48 CP]

---

### INTANGIBILITY [Variable]
Become non-solid in various ways.

**PHASING [30 CP]**
- Walk through solid matter
- Bonus action to activate, lasts until end of turn
- While phasing, can't attack or be attacked
- Can fall through floors if not careful
- 3 uses per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Combat Phasing (+15 CP):** Can phase as reaction when hit
  - **Affects Carried Items (+10%):** Equipment phases with you
  - **Affects Others (+50% per person):** Can make allies intangible by touching
  - **Requires Concentration (-20%):** Must focus to maintain
  - **Involuntary (-30%):** Can't fully control, phases randomly when stressed

**DENSITY CONTROL [60 CP]**
- Adjust body density as bonus action
- **Low Density:** Become intangible (as Phasing above)
- **High Density:** Become super-heavy (+4 STR, DR +10, can't fly/swim)
- Can switch freely between densities
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Extreme Density (+20 CP):** High density grants +8 STR, DR +15
  - **Partial Control (+15 CP):** Can alter density of body parts separately

**DIMENSION SHIFT [50 CP]**
- Shift to alternate dimension/phase
- Become completely untargetable and invisible
- Can observe normal dimension while shifted
- Lasts up to 1 minute
- 1 use per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Frequent Shifting (+20 CP):** 3 uses per short rest
  - **Combat Phase (+15 CP):** Can shift as reaction to being hit

**Examples:**
- Kitty Pryde: Phasing + Affects Carried Items + Combat Phasing [52 CP]
- Vision: Density Control + Extreme Density [80 CP]
- Nightcrawler: Dimension Shift (via teleportation) [part of Teleportation]

### REACTIVE DEFENSE [Variable]
Automatic counter when attacked.

**REACTIVE ARMOR [25 CP]**
- When hit by melee attack, attacker takes 1d6 damage
- Choose damage type (fire, cold, lightning, force, acid, etc.)
- Works automatically, no action required
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Scales with Damage (+15 CP):** Counter damage = half damage taken
  - **Area Effect (+20 CP):** Affects all adjacent enemies
  - **Specific Trigger Only (-30%):** Only vs fire, only vs melee, etc.

**EXPLOSIVE DEFENSE [35 CP]**
- When hit, 10 ft radius explosion deals 2d6 damage
- DEX save (DC 8 + Prof + CON) for half
- 3 uses per long rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Larger Blast (+10 CP):** 20 ft radius
  - **More Charges (+5 CP):** +1 use per long rest

**REFLECTION [40 CP]**
- When hit by ranged attack, can reflect it back at attacker (reaction)
- Attacker takes their own damage
- 3 uses per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Melee Reflection (+20 CP):** Also works on melee attacks
  - **Automatic (-0%):** No reaction needed, always active

**ABSORPTION [45 CP]**
- When hit by specific damage type, take half damage
- Add absorbed damage to your next attack (max = your level)
- Choose one damage type (fire, kinetic, energy, etc.)
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Multiple Types (+15 CP per type):** Absorb 2, 3, or more types
  - **Full Absorption (+20 CP):** Take no damage instead of half
  - **Rechargeable (+10 CP):** Store absorbed energy, use later

**Examples:**
- Human Torch: Reactive Armor (fire) [25 CP]
- Sebastian Shaw: Absorption (kinetic) + Full Absorption [65 CP]
- Bishop: Absorption (all energy) + Rechargeable [70 CP]

## MOBILITY ADVANTAGES

### ENHANCED MOVEMENT [Variable]
Move faster or in unusual ways.

**SUPER SPEED [10 CP per +20 ft]**
- Increase base speed by 20/40/60/80+ ft
- At 100+ ft speed, gain ability to run on water and up walls
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Lightning Speed (+30 CP):** At 200+ ft, can use Dash as bonus action
  - **Combat Speed (+15 CP):** Can make one extra attack per round
  - **Subsonic (+40 CP):** Reach near-sonic speeds (500+ ft)

**FLIGHT [15 CP for 30 ft base]**
- Fly at listed speed with good maneuverability
- Additional speed: +10 CP per +20 ft
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Supersonic (+30 CP):** Reach Mach 1+ speeds
  - **Space Flight (+40 CP):** Can fly in vacuum, no air needed
  - **Passengers (+20 CP per person):** Carry others while flying
  - **Winged (-5%):** Requires wings, can be bound/clipped
  - **Requires Running Start (-10%):** Can't hover, must stay in motion
  - **Specific Method (+0%):** Describe how you fly (wings, energy, etc.) - cosmetic only

**TUNNELING [20 CP for 20 ft]**
- Burrow through earth, stone, or similar material
- Can leave tunnel behind or fill it in (your choice)
- Additional speed: +10 CP per +10 ft
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Through Metal (+20 CP):** Can tunnel through metal
  - **Through Anything (+40 CP):** Can tunnel through any solid matter

**ENHANCED SWIMMING [5 CP per +20 ft swim speed]**
- Swim faster than normal
- At 60 ft+ swim speed, can breathe underwater
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Amphibious (+5 CP):** Can breathe air and water equally

**WALL-CRAWLING [20 CP]**
- Climb any surface at half normal speed
- Cannot be knocked off surfaces
- Can walk on ceilings
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Full Speed Climbing (+10 CP):** Climb at full movement speed
  - **Leave Trail (+10 CP):** Leave sticky residue others can use

**Examples:**
- Superman: Flight 80 ft, Supersonic, Space Flight [125 CP]
- Flash: Super Speed +200 ft, Lightning Speed, Combat Speed [245 CP]
- Spider-Man: Wall-Crawling + Full Speed [30 CP]

---

### TELEPORTATION [40 CP base]
Instantly move from one location to another.
- 100 ft range, line of sight required
- Move action to teleport (replaces movement)
- Can teleport as many times as you have movement
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Long Range (+20 CP):** 500 ft range
  - **Very Long Range (+40 CP):** 1 mile range
  - **Dimensional (+50 CP):** Can teleport to other dimensions/planes
  - **Portal Creation (+30 CP):** Leave portal behind for others
  - **Combat Teleport (+15 CP):** Can teleport as bonus action
  - **Affects Others (+25 CP per person):** Bring allies along
  - **Line of Sight Only (-10%):** Must see destination
  - **Requires Magic (-20%):** Part of magic system, costs FP
  - **Beacon Required (-30%):** Must have been to location before or have anchor point
  - **Loud/Flashy (-5%):** Creates noise/light/smoke, obvious
  - **Blink Step (+0%):** Describe visual effect - cosmetic

**Examples:**
- Nightcrawler: Dimensional + Loud (BAMF) + Affects Others (1 person) [115 CP]
- Doctor Strange: Dimensional + Portal Creation + Requires Magic [96 CP]
- Shadowstrike: Combat Teleport + Line of Sight [49 CP]

---

### PORTAL CREATION [45 CP]
Create stable gateways between two points.
- Both endpoints must be visible to you
- 500 ft max range between points
- Portal is Large size (10×10 ft)
- Lasts 1 minute
- 2 uses per long rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Large Portals (+15 CP):** Huge size (20×20 ft), vehicles can pass
  - **Distant (+25 CP):** 1 mile range
  - **Dimensional (+40 CP):** Connect to other planes of existence
  - **Permanent (+60 CP):** Portal lasts until you dismiss it
  - **One-Way (-10%):** Can only pass through one direction
  - **Two-Way (+0%):** Default, both directions work

**Examples:**
- Doctor Strange: Dimensional + Distant [110 CP]
- Blink: Large Portals + Two-Way [60 CP]

### SPECIAL MOVEMENT [Variable]

**WATER WALKING [10 CP]**
- Walk on liquid surfaces as if solid ground
- Move at normal speed across water

**AIR WALKING [15 CP]**
- Walk on air as if climbing invisible stairs
- Gain 20 ft vertical movement per turn
- Cannot hover (must keep "climbing")

**LEVITATION [25 CP]**
- Hover in place or move slowly (20 ft/round)
- Don't need solid ground
- Can avoid falling damage

**WEB-SLINGING [25 CP]**
- Generate sticky webbing from your body
- Can swing on webs (treat as Flight 40 ft with Limited Use)
- Can create web constructs (nets, shields, cocoons)
- Webs have 10 HP, AC 12, dissolve after 1 hour
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Organic (+0%):** Produced by body naturally
  - **Mechanical Webshooters (-10%):** Requires device, can run out of web fluid
  - **Enhanced Tensile Strength (+10 CP):** Webs have 20 HP, hold 5 tons
  - **Sticky Webs (+10 CP):** Grappled targets have disadvantage to escape
  - **Web Constructs (+15 CP):** Can create complex structures as action

**Examples:**
- Spider-Man: Web-Slinging + Enhanced Tensile + Sticky Webs [45 CP]
- Aquaman: Water Walking [10 CP]

## OFFENSIVE ADVANTAGES

### INNATE ATTACK [Variable]
Built-in attack capability (energy blast, breath weapon, eye beams, etc.).
- **Base Cost:** 1d6 damage = 6 CP
- **Scaling:** Each additional 1d6 = +6 CP
  - 2d6 = 12 CP
  - 3d6 = 18 CP
  - 4d6 = 24 CP
  - 5d6 = 30 CP
  - etc.
- **Range Options:**
  - Melee (reach 5 ft): Base cost
  - Ranged 60 ft: +0%
  - Ranged 120 ft: +10%
  - Ranged 300 ft: +30%
- **Damage Types:** Physical (piercing/slashing/bludgeoning), Energy (fire/cold/lightning/force/acid/thunder), Exotic (psychic/necrotic/radiant/poison)

**MODIFIERS:**
- **Area Effect (+50% per size increment):**
  - 10 ft radius: +50% (9 CP for 1d6 base)
  - 20 ft radius: +100% (12 CP for 1d6 base)
  - 30 ft radius: +150% (15 CP for 1d6 base)
- **Cone/Line (-10% from radius):** Same coverage but easier to avoid
  - 30 ft cone: +40%
  - 60 ft line: +90%
- **Armor Piercing (+50%):** Ignore half of target's DR and AC
- **Accurate (+10% per +1):** +1/+2/+3 to attack rolls
- **Inaccurate (-10% per -1):** -1 to hit, but costs less
- **Explosive (+20%):** Affects 5 ft radius on impact
- **Penetrating (+30%):** Continues through target, can hit enemies behind
- **Requires Ammunition (-10%):** Must reload/restock (arrows, bullets, etc.)
- **Requires Magic (-20%):** Costs FP to use, part of magical system
- **Linked to Attribute (+0%):** Damage adds STR/DEX/INT/WIS/CHA modifier
- **Surge (+20%):** Can spend FP to increase damage (1 FP = +1d6 for one attack)
- **Homing (+30%):** Attack has advantage to hit
- **Variable Damage Type (+15%):** Can choose damage type each use

**Examples:**
- Cyclops (concussive eye beam): 6d6, 120 ft range, Surge = 72 CP
- Human Torch (fire blast): 5d6, 60 ft range, Area 20 ft = 90 CP
- Superman (heat vision): 4d6, 300 ft range, Accurate +2 = 57 CP
- Dragon (breath weapon): 8d6, 30 ft cone, fire damage = 67 CP

## SENSORY ADVANTAGES

### ENHANCED SENSES [Variable]

**ACUTE SENSES [5 CP per +2 bonus]**
- **Acute Hearing:** +2/+4/+6 to hearing-based Perception checks
- **Acute Vision:** +2/+4/+6 to sight-based Perception checks
- **Acute Smell/Taste:** +2/+4/+6 to scent-based Perception checks
- Can stack multiple types

**SPECIAL VISION [Variable]**
- **Dark Vision** [10 CP]: See in darkness as if dim light (60 ft)
  - **Extended Range (+5 CP):** 120 ft range
- **X-Ray Vision** [30 CP]: See through solid objects (can't see through lead or magical barriers)
- **Telescopic Vision** [15 CP]: See details at extreme distances (miles away)
- **Microscopic Vision** [15 CP]: See tiny details invisible to normal eyes
- **Infrared Vision** [20 CP]: See heat signatures through darkness/smoke
- **Ultraviolet Vision** [15 CP]: See UV spectrum, track fluorescent trails
- **360° Vision** [20 CP]: See in all directions simultaneously, can't be flanked

**EXOTIC SENSES [Variable]**
- **Danger Sense** [15 CP]: Cannot be surprised, +2 to Initiative, sixth sense warns of threats
- **Tremorsense** [20 CP]: Detect vibrations through ground/walls within 30 ft
- **Blindsight** [30 CP]: "See" without eyes using echolocation/vibrations/other senses (30 ft radius)
- **Truesight** [40 CP]: See through illusions, invisibility, magical darkness, and shapechanging (60 ft)
- **Detect [Specific]** [10 CP]: Detect magic, lies, emotions, evil, technology, etc.
  - Specify what you detect
  - 30 ft radius

**MODIFIERS:**
- **Extended Range (+50%):** Double or triple range
- **Targeting (+10 CP):** Can use sense to guide attacks (ignore concealment penalties)
- **Always Active (+5 CP):** Sense is always on, can't turn off
- **Requires Concentration (-10%):** Must focus to use

**Examples:**
- Daredevil: Blindsight 60 ft + Acute Hearing +6 + Acute Smell +4 [80 CP]
- Superman: X-Ray + Telescopic + Microscopic [60 CP]
- Wolverine: Acute Smell +6 + Tracking [25 CP]

## CONTROL & MANIPULATION ADVANTAGES

### ELEMENTAL CONTROL [Variable - usually class-based]
Control and manipulate elemental forces.
- **Elements:** Fire, Water, Air, Earth, Ice, Lightning, Metal, Nature, Light, Darkness
- Usually provided by Conduit class, but can be purchased separately
- **Base Package includes:**
  - Innate Attack (element)
  - Immunity to element
  - Basic manipulation

**ADDITIONAL CONTROL POWERS [40 CP each]:**
- **Create/Shape Element:** Form element from nothing, reshape existing matter
- **Animate Element:** Make element move and attack on its own
- **Absorb Element:** Heal HP by consuming your element
- **Transmute to Element:** Turn objects into your element

**MODIFIERS:**
- **Massive Scale (+50%):** Control element in 300 ft radius instead of 60 ft
- **Precise Control (+20%):** Extremely fine manipulation (sculpt ice, write with fire, etc.)
- **Requires Gesture (-10%):** Must make hand motions to control
- **Requires Concentration (-20%):** Can't multitask while controlling

**Examples:**
- Storm (weather/lightning): Air/Lightning Control + Massive Scale [120 CP]
- Iceman (ice): Ice Control + Create/Shape + Transmute [120 CP]

---

### MAGNETISM CONTROL [80 CP]
Control metal objects via magnetic force.
- Lift and move up to 10 tons of metal within 120 ft
- Create magnetic force fields (as Force Field advantage)
- Detect metal through walls (60 ft range)
- Flight via magnetic levitation (60 ft speed)
- Can pull/push metal objects
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Planetary Scale (+50 CP):** Affect Earth's entire magnetic field
  - **EMP (+20 CP):** Disable all electronics in 60 ft radius, 1/long rest
  - **Molecular Manipulation (+40 CP):** Reshape metal at atomic level
  - **Ferrous Metals Only (-20%):** Only affects iron/steel, not all metals

**Examples:**
- Magneto: Planetary + EMP + Molecular Manipulation [190 CP]
- Polaris: Base magnetism [80 CP]

---

### GRAVITY MANIPULATION [60 CP]
Increase or decrease gravitational force.
- Affect 30 ft radius
- Make objects weightless or extremely heavy
- Flight by negating own gravity (40 ft speed)
- Crush attack: 3d8 force damage (STR save for half)
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Personal Only (-30 CP):** Only affect yourself (flight + enhanced jumping)
  - **Singularity (+30 CP):** Create miniature black holes
  - **Anti-Gravity Field (+15 CP):** Allies in radius can fly

**Examples:**
- Graviton: Full gravity control [60 CP]

### TELEKINESIS [Variable]
Move objects with your mind.
- **Base Cost by Weight Limit:**
  - Light (5 lbs) [15 CP]
  - Medium (50 lbs) [25 CP]
  - Heavy (500 lbs) [40 CP]
  - Massive (1 ton) [60 CP]
  - Extreme (10 tons) [100 CP]
- Range: 60 ft
- Move action to move object
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Combat TK (+30%):** Can use to attack (counts as Innate Attack)
  - **Flight (+20%):** Can fly by moving self telekinetically
  - **Fine Manipulation (+15%):** Can pick locks, type on keyboard, perform surgery
  - **Improved Range (+10% per doubling):** 120 ft, 240 ft, 480 ft, etc.
  - **Multiple Objects (+15% per additional):** Lift 2, 3, or more objects simultaneously

**Examples:**
- Jean Grey: Extreme + Combat TK + Flight [156 CP]
- Mentalist (basic): Heavy + Fine Manipulation [46 CP]

### MIND CONTROL [Variable]
Influence or control other minds.

**TELEPATHY [30 CP]**
- Read surface thoughts (what they're actively thinking)
- Send your own thoughts to others
- Range: 60 ft
- Target must make WIS save to resist
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Extended Range (+10 CP per doubling):** 120 ft, 240 ft, unlimited
  - **Mass Telepathy (+20 CP):** Affect up to 10 minds simultaneously
  - **Telepathic Network (+20 CP):** Link multiple minds so they can all communicate

**MIND READING [30 CP]**
- Read deep thoughts, memories, secrets
- Requires physical touch
- Target makes WIS save to resist
- Takes 1 minute of contact to read thoroughly

**MIND CONTROL [50 CP]**
- Command a creature's actions
- Target makes WIS save to resist
- Lasts 1 minute if successful
- Can give complex commands
- 1 use per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Mass Control (+30 CP per target):** Control 2, 3, or more creatures
  - **Permanent Control (+60 CP):** Control lasts until dispelled
  - **Subtle (+15 CP):** Victim doesn't know they're controlled

**EMOTION CONTROL [35 CP]**
- Inflict specific emotion (fear, rage, calm, love, etc.)
- Range: 60 ft
- Target makes WIS save
- Lasts 10 minutes
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Area Effect (+20 CP):** Affect 20 ft radius

**POSSESSION [70 CP]**
- Take over another creature's body completely
- Your body becomes unconscious and helpless
- Control target's actions fully
- Target gets WIS save to resist
- Lasts until you release them or are forced out
- 1 use per long rest

**MODIFIERS (apply to all):**
- **Requires Eye Contact (-10%):** Must see target's eyes
- **Requires Touch (-20%):** Must physically touch target
- **Vow (Never invade innocent minds) [-10 CP]:** Ethical self-limitation

**Examples:**
- Professor X: Telepathy + Mind Reading + Mind Control + Mass Control (10+) + Extended Range (unlimited) [200+ CP]
- Empath: Emotion Control + Area Effect [55 CP]

### ANIMAL/PLANT/OBJECT CONTROL [Variable]

**ANIMAL CONTROL [30 CP]**
- Command animals within 60 ft
- Communicate with animals
- Give simple commands
- Share senses with animals
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Specific Type Only (-20%):** Only insects, only aquatic, only mammals, etc.
  - **Massive Swarm (+20 CP):** Control hundreds or thousands simultaneously
  - **Combat Training (+10 CP):** Animals fight effectively for you

**PLANT CONTROL [40 CP]**
- Command plants within 60 ft
- Cause rapid growth (1 action, plants grow to full size)
- Entangle enemies with vines/roots
- Create plant structures (walls, bridges)
- Communicate with plant life
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Massive Scale (+20 CP):** 300 ft radius
  - **Combat Plants (+15 CP):** Plants can attack (2d6 damage)
  - **Plant Form (+25 CP):** Transform into plant matter yourself

**OBJECT ANIMATION [45 CP]**
- Bring inanimate objects to life
- Animate up to 5 objects within 60 ft
- Objects obey your commands
- Lasts 10 minutes
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Permanent (+30 CP):** Objects stay animated indefinitely
  - **Large Objects (+20 CP):** Can animate vehicles, buildings, etc.

**Examples:**
- Aquaman: Animal Control (aquatic only) + Massive Swarm [40 CP]
- Poison Ivy: Plant Control + Combat Plants + Massive Scale [95 CP]
- Ant-Man: Animal Control (insects only) + Massive Swarm [40 CP]

### PROBABILITY MANIPULATION [50 CP]
Alter luck and chance.
- Once per short rest: Force enemy to reroll attack (take worse result)
- Once per short rest: Reroll your own roll (take better result)
- Passive aura: Allies within 30 ft gain +1 to all rolls
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Good Luck Only (-10 CP):** Can only help, not hinder enemies
  - **Bad Luck Aura (+15 CP):** Enemies within 30 ft have -1 to all rolls
  - **Hexes (+20 CP):** Can target specific enemy with persistent bad luck (disadvantage)
  - **Unconscious Only (-20 CP):** Only passive effect, no conscious control

**Examples:**
- Domino: Good Luck Only [45 CP]
- Scarlet Witch: Full probability manipulation [50 CP]
- Longshot: Good Luck + Hexes [75 CP]

---

### REALITY WARPING [150+ CP]
**WARNING: COSMIC-TIER ONLY**
Alter reality itself at fundamental level.
- Can create or destroy matter
- Change physical laws temporarily
- Rewrite recent events
- Transmute anything into anything else
- **This is extremely powerful and usually reserved for cosmic-tier campaigns or as limited MacGuffin powers**

**Examples:**
- Scarlet Witch (full power): Reality Warping [200+ CP]
- Franklin Richards: Reality Warping [300+ CP]

---

### TIME MANIPULATION [Variable]
**WARNING: NARRATIVE-BREAKING**
Manipulate the flow of time.
- **Slow Time** [60 CP]: Slow time in 30 ft radius for 1 minute (enemies halved), 1/long rest
- **Speed Self** [60 CP]: Speed up own time (Haste effect on self), 1/long rest
- **Time Stop** [100 CP]: Stop time for 1 round (only you can act), 1/day
- **Rewind** [80 CP]: Undo the last round (after seeing results), 1/day

**Only allow in games where time shenanigans are part of the theme!**

## TRANSFORMATION ADVANTAGES

### SHAPESHIFTING [Variable]
Change your physical form.

**MORPH (Appearance Only) [20 CP]**
- Change appearance to any humanoid form you've seen
- Voice mimicry included
- Clothes and equipment don't change (need to change separately)
- No change to stats or abilities
- **Examples:** Mystique (basic), disguise specialists

**ALTERNATE FORM [40 CP]**
- One specific preset alternate form
- Can have different physical stats (STR/DEX/CON)
- Can have different size category
- Bonus action to transform
- Lasts 1 hour
- 3 uses per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Multiple Forms (+20 CP per form):** Have 2, 3, 4+ different forms
  - **Enhanced Stats (+10 CP):** Forms grant +3 to stats instead of +2
  - **Unlimited Duration (+15 CP):** Forms last until you change back
  - **Instant Change (+5 CP):** Transform as free action

**FULL SHAPESHIFTING [80 CP]**
- Transform into any creature you've seen
- Copy appearance and voice perfectly
- Can approximate abilities with GM approval (must buy with CP)
- Bonus action to transform
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Animal Forms Only (-20%):** Can only become animals, not humanoids
  - **Objects (+30 CP):** Can become furniture, vehicles, weapons, etc.
  - **Size Change (+20 CP):** Can dramatically change size categories
  - **Partial Transformation (+10 CP):** Transform only parts of your body
  - **Copy Abilities (+50 CP):** Actually gain abilities of creature you copy (limited)

**MODIFIERS (all types):**
- **Requires Concentration (-10%):** Must focus to maintain form
- **Monstrous Appearance (true form) [-10 CP]:** Your natural form is disturbing
- **Stuck in Form (-20%):** Difficult to return to natural form (requires rest, magic, etc.)

**Examples:**
- Mystique: Morph [20 CP]
- Beast Boy: Full Shapeshifting (animals only) [64 CP]
- Metamorph class: Alternate Form (multiple) [60+ CP]

---

### SIZE CHANGE [50 CP]
Grow or shrink your body.
- Change one size category (Medium ↔ Small or Large) as bonus action
- **When Larger:** +4 STR, +2 CON, -2 DEX
- **When Smaller:** -4 STR, +4 DEX, can fit through tiny spaces
- Lasts up to 1 hour
- 2 uses per short rest
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Extreme Growth (+20 CP):** Can become Huge (15 ft tall)
  - **Extreme Shrinking (+20 CP):** Can become Tiny (6 inches)
  - **Giant Form (+30 CP):** Can become Gargantuan (30+ ft tall)
  - **Microscopic (+25 CP):** Shrink to subatomic size
  - **Mass Constant (+15 CP):** Weight/mass doesn't change with size
  - **Permanent Form (-20%):** Stuck at one size, can't change back easily

**Examples:**
- Ant-Man: Extreme Shrinking + Microscopic + Mass Constant [110 CP]
- Giant-Man: Extreme Growth + Giant Form [100 CP]
- Wasp: Extreme Shrinking + Flight [70 CP]

---

### DUPLICATION [40 CP]
Create copies of yourself.
- Create 1 duplicate as action
- Duplicate has your stats but half HP
- Both you and duplicate act on your turn
- Duplicate lasts 10 minutes or until reduced to 0 HP
- **Modifiers:**
  - **Multiple Duplicates (+20 CP per additional):** Create 2, 3, 5, 10, 100+ duplicates
  - **Illusory Only (-15 CP):** Duplicates have no HP, vanish when hit, can't deal damage
  - **Permanent Splits (+30 CP):** Duplicates last indefinitely until dismissed
  - **Mental Link (+10 CP):** Share senses and thoughts with duplicates perfectly
  - **Independent (+15 CP):** Duplicates act on their own initiative

**Examples:**
- Multiple Man: Multiple Duplicates (100+) + Permanent Splits + Independent [150+ CP]
- Naruto (shadow clones): Multiple Duplicates (20) + Illusory [65 CP]
- Loki (illusions): Duplication + Illusory Only [34 CP]
