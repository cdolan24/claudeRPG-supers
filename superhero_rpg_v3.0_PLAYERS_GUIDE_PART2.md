# ADVANTAGES

Advantages are special abilities, powers, and enhancements you purchase with Character Points (CP). They define what makes your hero super.

## How Advantages Work

**Purchasing**: Spend CP to buy advantages  
**Stacking**: Most advantages can be purchased multiple times (Enhanced STR +1, +2, +3, etc.)  
**Class Synergy**: Some advantages cost less for specific classes (listed in description)  
**Restrictions**: Some advantages have minimum level requirements (enforced by Power Tier system)

## Advantage Categories

1. **Physical Enhancements** - Improve body and physical abilities
2. **Mental Enhancements** - Improve mind and mental abilities
3. **Combat Abilities** - Improve fighting effectiveness
4. **Movement Abilities** - Enhanced mobility and speed
5. **Defensive Abilities** - Protection and survival
6. **Offensive Powers** - Attack abilities and damage
7. **Sensory Abilities** - Enhanced senses and perception
8. **Utility Powers** - Versatile and situational abilities
9. **Social Abilities** - Interaction and influence
10. **Healing & Recovery** - Restore HP and remove conditions

## PHYSICAL ENHANCEMENTS

### **Enhanced Strength +1** [10 CP per level]
Increase STR by +1 (maximum +10). Each +1 = +1 to hit, +1 damage, better carrying capacity.
- **Stacks**: Yes (can buy multiple times)
- **Example**: Enhanced STR +4 [40 CP] = STR increases by 4

### **Enhanced Dexterity +1** [10 CP per level]
Increase DEX by +1 (maximum +10). Each +1 = +1 AC, +1 to hit (ranged/finesse), +1 initiative.
- **Stacks**: Yes

### **Enhanced Constitution +1** [10 CP per level]
Increase CON by +1 (maximum +10). Each +1 = +2 HP per level.
- **Stacks**: Yes

### **Enhanced Move 1** [20 CP per level, max 5]
Multiply base speed. Level 1 = ×2 (60 ft), Level 2 = ×4 (120 ft), Level 3 = ×8 (240 ft), Level 4 = ×16 (480 ft), Level 5 = ×32 (960 ft).
- **Speedster Class**: Free Level 1 at 1st, gains +1 every 5 levels
- **Stacks**: Yes (up to 5 levels)

### **Regeneration** [20 CP per level, max 3]
Regain HP automatically. Level 1 = 1 HP/turn, Level 2 = 2 HP/turn, Level 3 = 3 HP/turn (in combat) or per minute (out of combat).
- **Limitations**: Doesn't work if took fire/acid damage last round, stops at 0 HP
- **Example**: Wolverine has Regeneration 3

### **Tough as Nails** [15 CP per level, max 3]
Heal extra HP when spending Hit Dice. Level 1 = +1 HP/die, Level 2 = +2 HP/die, Level 3 = +3 HP/die.
- **Applies to**: Short rests, Heroic Recovery, Second Wind

### **Ambidexterity** [5 CP]
No penalties for using off-hand. Can dual-wield without disadvantage.

### **Catfall** [10 CP]
Reduce fall damage by 10 × character level. Always land on feet.
- **Example**: Level 5 = reduce fall damage by 50 ft

### **High Pain Threshold** [10 CP]
Advantage on saves vs pain effects. Can function normally while taking ongoing damage.

### **Immunity (Specific)** [20 CP]
Immune to one specific condition or damage type. Choose: poison, disease, fire, cold, etc.
- **Stacks**: Each immunity purchased separately

Note: This is technically a disadvantage but often taken voluntarily

## COMBAT ABILITIES

### **Combat Reflexes** [15 CP]
+1 to initiative, never surprised while conscious.

### **Danger Sense** [15 CP]
Can't be surprised while conscious. Advantage on initiative rolls.
- **Stacks with**: Combat Reflexes (total +4 to initiative)

### **Weapon Master (Specific)** [20 CP]
Choose weapon type. Gain expertise (double proficiency), critical hits on 19-20 with that weapon.
- **Example**: Weapon Master (Katana), Weapon Master (Escrima Sticks)
- **Martial Artist Discount**: 20 CP for class members, 40 CP for others

### **Enhanced Active Defense** [10 CP per level, max 3]
Add +1/+2/+3 to your Active Defense reaction (on top of ability modifier + proficiency).
- **Total Active Defense**: Ability modifier + Proficiency + this bonus

### **Precise Strike** [20 CP]
Once per turn, reroll one attack roll. Must use new result.

### **Defensive Attack** [15 CP]
Bonus action, gain +2 AC until start of next turn, but all attacks have disadvantage this turn.

### **All-Out Attack** [15 CP]
Bonus action, gain +2 to all attack rolls until start of next turn, but AC -2 and can't use reactions.

### **Rapid Strike** [25 CP]
Once per short rest, make one additional attack as part of Attack action (beyond Extra Attack).

### **Cleave** [20 CP]
When you reduce enemy to 0 HP with melee attack, can immediately attack adjacent enemy (same attack roll).

### **Combat Expertise** [20 CP]
When you take defensive stance (bonus action), gain +4 AC instead of normal Dodge (+2).

## MOVEMENT ABILITIES

### **Flight** [40 CP]
Fly speed 60 ft. Can hover.
- **Alternative**: Enhanced Move can represent super-speed ground movement

### **Wallcrawling** [15 CP]
Move along walls and ceilings at normal speed. Don't need hands free.

### **Swinging** [10 CP]
Move by swinging from objects (webs, grappling hook). Add 20 ft to speed when swinging.

### **Swimming** [10 CP]
Swim speed = walking speed. Can breathe underwater.

### **Burrowing** [20 CP]
Burrow through earth/stone at half speed. Leave tunnel or collapse behind you (your choice).

### **Teleportation** [30 CP per level, max 3]
Bonus action, teleport to visible location. Level 1 = 30 ft, Level 2 = 60 ft, Level 3 = 100 ft. Uses per short rest = proficiency bonus.
- **Trickster Class**: First level costs 20 CP for class members

### **Dimensional Rift** [50 CP]
**Requires**: 11+ levels, Speedster or Mystic class
Action, create portal between two points within 100 ft. Lasts 1 minute. 1/long rest.

### **Perfect Balance** [15 CP]
Never fall prone involuntarily. Can walk on narrow surfaces without checks. Advantage on Acrobatics.

### **Parkour Master** [20 CP]
Climbing and jumping don't cost extra movement. Can long jump up to STR × 3 feet.

## DEFENSIVE ABILITIES

### **Physical Resistance** [30 CP]
Resistance to physical damage (half damage from bludgeoning, slashing, piercing).

### **Energy Resistance** [30 CP]
Resistance to energy damage (half damage from fire, cold, lightning, radiant, necrotic).

### **Damage Resistance (Specific)** [10 CP each]
Resistance to one specific type. Choose: fire, cold, lightning, acid, poison, force, psychic, radiant, necrotic.

### **Damage Reduction** [Not Recommended - Use Resistance Instead]
*v3.0 Note: DR removed in favor of simpler Resistance system*

### **Force Field** [30 CP per level, max 3]
Reaction when hit, gain +3 AC vs that attack (Level 1). Uses/combat = proficiency bonus.
- **Level 2** [60 CP total]: +4 AC
- **Level 3** [90 CP total]: +5 AC

### **Invisibility** [40 CP]
Action, become invisible 10 minutes (concentration). Attack/cast ends early. Prof times/long rest.
- **Greater Invisibility** [80 CP]: Attacking doesn't end invisibility. Requires 11+ levels.

### **Intangibility** [50 CP]
**Requires**: 11+ levels
Action, become incorporeal 1 minute. Can pass through solid matter, immune to physical damage, can't attack. 1/long rest.
- **Speedster Class**: Pay 50 CP (others pay 100 CP)

### **Tough Skin** [20 CP]
Natural armor. AC = 13 + DEX modifier (no armor needed).
- **Tough Skin 2** [40 CP total]: AC = 15 + DEX modifier

### **Armored Form** [30 CP]
When transformed or in powered state, gain +2 AC.

### **Reflective Shield** [40 CP]
When you use Active Defense to deflect attack, can reflect ranged attack back at attacker (same attack roll, your damage).

## OFFENSIVE POWERS

### **Innate Attack** [12 CP per die, max initial 5d6]
Ranged attack, 60 ft, deal energy damage (choose type: fire, cold, lightning, force, etc.).
- **Example**: Innate Attack (Fire) 3d6 [36 CP] = +spell attack to hit, 3d6 fire damage
- **Scaling**: Can purchase additional dice at 12 CP each
- **Limitation**: Street level (1-4) max 3d6, Superheroic (5-10) max 6d6, Cosmic (11+) no max

### **Infinite Mass Punch** [40 CP]
**Requires**: 5+ Speedster levels, DEX 18+, Enhanced Move 3+
Once per short rest, attack deals +5d6 force damage and target is stunned (CON save DC 8 + Prof + DEX).
- **Speedster Class**: 40 CP, Others: 80 CP

### **Stunning Attack** [20 CP]
Once per short rest when you hit with melee attack, target must make CON save (DC 8 + Prof + STR/DEX) or be stunned 1 round.

### **Power Attack** [10 CP]
Before attack roll, take -2 to hit for +4 damage. Decide before rolling.

### **Sneak Attack** [Martial Artist/Trickster class feature only]
*Note: Not purchasable as advantage, gained through class levels*

### **Area Attack (Cone)** [25 CP]
Choose element type. Action, 30 ft cone, 3d6 damage, DEX save DC (8 + Prof + ability) for half. 3 FP cost. Can cast Prof times/long rest.

### **Area Attack (Radius)** [30 CP]
Choose element type. Action, 20 ft radius within 60 ft, 3d6 damage, DEX save for half. 3 FP cost. Can cast Prof times/long rest.

### **Beam Attack (Line)** [20 CP]
Choose element type. Action, 5 ft wide × 60 ft line, 3d6 damage, DEX save for half. 2 FP cost. Can cast Prof times/long rest.

### **Explosive Attack** [35 CP]
When you hit with ranged attack, can spend bonus action to make attack explode. All creatures within 10 ft take half the rolled damage (DEX save for quarter). 1/short rest.

### **Continuing Damage** [15 CP]
Choose one attack you have. On hit, target takes ongoing 1d6 damage at start of their turn (same type as attack). Ends if they use action to extinguish/remove. Lasts 1 minute.

Transform into specific form (humanoid, beast, etc.). Takes 1 action. Lasts hours = level.
- **Metamorph Class**: Gets multiple forms free through class features

### **Morph (Limited)** [20 CP]
Change appearance to any humanoid of similar size. Don't gain abilities. Lasts hours = level.

### **Size Alteration** [25 CP per direction]
Grow to Large (+2 STR, +1d4 damage) or Shrink to Tiny (+2 DEX, advantage Stealth). Bonus action. Prof times/long rest. 10 minutes duration.
- **Both Directions** [50 CP]: Can grow OR shrink

### **Telepathy (Basic)** [20 CP]
Read surface thoughts of creature within 30 ft (WIS save DC 8 + Prof + WIS/CHA). Can send mental messages.

### **Telekinesis (Basic)** [25 CP]
Lift and move objects up to 100 lbs within 30 ft as bonus action. Can attack with lifted object (+ability to hit, 1d8 + ability damage).

### **Mind Link** [30 CP]
Create telepathic connection with up to 6 willing creatures within 1 mile. Communicate mentally. Lasts 8 hours.

### **Illusion (Minor)** [15 CP]
Create minor illusion (sound, image, or smell) within 30 ft. Lasts 1 minute. At will.

### **Illusion (Major)** [40 CP]
Create convincing illusion covering 20 ft cube. Sight, sound, smell, temperature. Lasts 10 minutes (concentration). 3/long rest.

### **Gadgeteer** [20 CP]
+4 bonus to all Technology and Engineering checks. Can create jury-rigged devices during downtime.

### **Master Hacker** [25 CP]
Advantage on all hacking attempts. Can hack secure systems in half time. Access systems remotely within 100 ft.

### **Natural Linguist** [15 CP]
Learn new language with 1 week immersion. Advantage on Insight checks to understand unfamiliar languages/codes.

### **Wealth (Level)** [20 CP per level, max 3]
Level 1 = $100k/year income, Level 2 = $1M/year, Level 3 = $10M+/year. Never need to worry about common expenses.

## HEALING & RECOVERY

### **Healing Touch** [30 CP per level, max 2]
**Requires**: CHA 13+ or WIS 13+
Action, touch creature, spend 3 FP (Level 1) or 5 FP (Level 2), heal 2d6 + modifier (Level 1) or 4d6 + modifier (Level 2). Uses = Prof/long rest.

### **Fast Healing** [30 CP]
When you regain HP from any source, add +1 HP per die rolled.
- **Example**: Cure Wounds 2d8+4 becomes 2d8+4+2 (for 2 dice)

### **Metabolic Control** [25 CP]
Once per long rest, spend 1 minute concentrating. Heal HP = your CON score (full score, not modifier).
- **Example**: CON 16 = heal 16 HP

### **Life Link** [40 CP]
**Requires**: CHA 15+
Bonus action, choose ally within 30 ft. When they take damage, you can use reaction to take half. When you heal, they heal half. Lasts 1 hour. 2/long rest.

### **Undying** [50 CP]
**Requires**: CON 15+
When reduced to 0 HP, make CON save DC 15. Success = drop to 1 HP instead. 1/long rest.

### **Relentless** [40 CP]
**Requires**: CON 15+
When reduced to 0 HP, make CON save DC 10 (increases by 5 for each additional use in same combat). Success = drop to 1 HP and immediately make one melee attack. 1/long rest.

### **Last Stand** [35 CP]
**Requires**: CON 15+
When reduced to 0 HP, can choose to stay conscious. Gain temp HP = CON score (full score). Continue acting. When temp HP depleted, fall unconscious as normal. 1/long rest.

### **Berserker Rage** [30 CP]
Bonus action, enter rage 1 minute. +2 melee damage, resistance to physical damage, advantage on STR checks/saves. Can't cast spells or concentrate. 2/long rest (increases by 1 at levels 6, 12, 18).

### **Battle Fury** [20 CP]
When you reduce enemy to 0 HP, gain temp HP = 1d6 + CON modifier. Can gain multiple times per combat.

**Requires**: CON 13+

### **Adrenaline Surge** [25 CP]
Bonus action, 2/long rest. Gain extra action this turn (can use to Attack, Dash, or Use Object, not cast spell). After using, take 1d6 force damage (exhaustion).

### **Frenzy** [45 CP]
**Requires**: Berserker Rage or similar
While raging, can make one additional melee attack as bonus action each turn. After rage ends, gain 1 level exhaustion.

### **War Cry** [20 CP]
**Requires**: CHA 13+
Action, let out primal scream. All enemies within 30 ft must make WIS save DC (8 + Prof + CHA) or be frightened 1 minute (can repeat save at end of turns). 2/long rest.

# DISADVANTAGES

Disadvantages are flaws, limitations, and weaknesses that grant you extra CP. Taking disadvantages allows you to buy more advantages or equipment.

## How Disadvantages Work

**Gaining CP**: Disadvantages grant CP (shown as negative cost: -10 CP means you GAIN 10 CP)  
**Maximum**: Can gain maximum 50 CP from disadvantages  
**Roleplaying**: You must actually roleplay these disadvantages. GM can veto if you ignore them.  
**Removal**: Can buy off disadvantage by spending 2× the CP it granted (GM approval)

---

## PHYSICAL DISADVANTAGES

### **Bad Sight** [-10 CP]
Disadvantage on Perception checks relying on sight beyond 30 ft. Need glasses/contacts (can be lost).

### **One Eye** [-15 CP]
Disadvantage on ranged attacks and Perception checks relying on sight. Missing eye is obvious.

### **One Arm** [-20 CP]
Missing arm. Can't use two-handed weapons, disadvantage on Athletics checks requiring two hands.

### **Lame** [-15 CP]
Speed reduced by 10 ft. Disadvantage on Acrobatics and Athletics checks involving legs.

### **Mute** [-15 CP]
Cannot speak. Must communicate through sign language, writing, or telepathy (if available).

### **Deaf** [-10 CP]
Can't hear. Disadvantage on Perception checks relying on hearing. Immune to sound-based effects.

### **Colorblind** [-5 CP]
Can only see in shades of grey. Some visual tasks are more difficult.

### **Weak Immune System** [-10 CP]
Disadvantage on saves vs disease and poison. Recover from illness at half normal rate.

### **Chronic Pain** [-10 CP]
Suffer ongoing pain from old injury. Disadvantage on Constitution saves. Pain can be distracting during stress.

### **Addiction (Substance)** [-10 to -20 CP]
Addicted to substance (drugs, alcohol, specific food). Must indulge daily or suffer withdrawal (disadvantage on all checks). Strong addiction (heroin, etc.) = -20 CP, mild (caffeine) = -10 CP.

### **Albinism** [-5 CP]
Extremely pale, sensitive to sunlight. Disadvantage on Stealth in daylight. Take extra 1d6 damage from sunlight exposure for prolonged periods.

---

## MENTAL DISADVANTAGES

### **Bad Temper** [-10 CP]
Quick to anger. When provoked, make WIS save DC 12 or fly into rage (must attack provoker). -2 to Persuasion and Deception when angry.

### **Berserk** [-15 CP]
When reduced below 25% HP, must make WIS save DC 15 or go berserk. Attack nearest creature (ally or enemy) until combat ends or you're knocked out.

### **Bloodlust** [-10 CP]
In combat, must make WIS save DC 12 each round after first blood is drawn or attack enemy relentlessly (can't use defensive abilities, can't retreat).

### **Compulsive Behavior** [-5 to -15 CP]
Must perform specific behavior. Minor (wash hands frequently) = -5 CP, Major (count everything, touch objects in pattern) = -10 CP, Severe (interferes with adventuring) = -15 CP.

### **Cowardice** [-10 CP]
When facing obviously superior enemy, must make WIS save DC 13 or flee/surrender. -2 to saves vs fear.

### **Curiosity** [-5 to -10 CP]
Must investigate mysteries. Make WIS save DC 12 or investigate instead of proceeding cautiously. Severe (must investigate regardless of danger) = -10 CP.

### **Fanaticism** [-10 CP]
Devoted to cause/deity/ideology to extreme degree. Will endanger self and others for cause.

### **Gullibility** [-10 CP]
Disadvantage on Insight checks to detect lies. Believe improbable stories unless they directly contradict known facts.

### **Honesty** [-10 CP]
Cannot lie convincingly. Disadvantage on Deception checks. Will not lie even to save lives (must roleplay how character justifies this).

### **Impulsiveness** [-10 CP]
Act before thinking. Make WIS save DC 13 or act immediately on first idea without planning. -2 to initiative (act last while you rush in).

### **Jealousy** [-10 CP]
Envious of others' success. When ally succeeds spectacularly or receives praise, must make WIS save DC 12 or become hostile/sullen.

### **Lecherous** [-10 CP]
Cannot resist attractive people. Make WIS save DC 12 or be distracted/make poor decisions around attractive targets.

### **Megalomania** [-10 CP]
Believe you are destined for greatness. Cannot accept being second-best. Must be leader or become resentful.

### **Nightmares** [-5 CP]
Suffer nightmares. Long rest only restores ¾ of HP and resources (round down).

### **Obsession** [-10 CP]
Obsessed with specific goal (revenge, finding artifact, proving theory). Will pursue at expense of other concerns. Make risky decisions when obsession is involved.

### **Overconfidence** [-5 CP]
Believe you can handle anything. Make WIS save DC 13 when facing obviously dangerous situation or charge in anyway.

### **Paranoia** [-10 CP]
Trust no one. Disadvantage on Persuasion checks. Always assume worst intentions. Must check for traps/ambushes constantly even when none exist.

### **Phobia** [-5 to -15 CP]
Irrational fear of specific thing. Minor (snakes, heights) = -5 CP, Major (fire, water) = -10 CP, Severe (common thing like crowds, enclosed spaces) = -15 CP. When encountering phobia, make WIS save DC 14 or be frightened.

### **Truthfulness** [-5 CP]
Strong aversion to lying. Can lie when absolutely necessary but suffer guilt. Disadvantage on Deception checks.

## SOCIAL DISADVANTAGES

### **Code of Honor (Hero's)** [-15 CP]
Will not kill (except in extreme circumstances), will protect innocents even at personal risk, will honor promises, will not torture.

### **Code of Honor (Chivalry)** [-15 CP]
Protect women and children, keep word, fight honorably, protect the weak, respect enemies.

### **Code of Honor (Pirate's)** [-10 CP]
Never betray crew, honor flag, fair share of loot, no fighting among crew.

### **Duty** [-5 to -15 CP]
Owe duty to organization. Minor (on call, 6 days/month) = -5 CP, Serious (12 days/month) = -10 CP, Extreme (always on call) = -15 CP.

### **Enemy** [-5 to -20 CP]
Have powerful enemy who seeks to harm you. Minor (individual, appears rarely) = -5 CP, Serious (organization, appears occasionally) = -10 CP, Major (powerful organization, appears frequently) = -20 CP.

### **Intolerance** [-5 to -10 CP]
Prejudice against specific group. Will not cooperate or help them. Minor (specific profession) = -5 CP, Major (entire race/species) = -10 CP.

### **Loner** [-5 CP]
Uncomfortable in groups. -2 to Persuasion and Deception checks when working with others. Prefer to work alone.

### **Low Empathy** [-10 CP]
Difficulty understanding others' emotions. -2 to Insight checks. May say insensitive things.

### **Reputation (Bad)** [-5 to -15 CP]
Known for negative trait. Minor (rude, cheap) = -5 CP, Serious (untrustworthy, violent) = -10 CP, Extreme (villain, war criminal) = -15 CP. Disadvantage on social checks with those who know reputation.

### **Secret** [-5 to -20 CP]
Harbor dangerous secret. Minor (embarrassing) = -5 CP, Serious (would lose job/friends) = -10 CP, Extreme (would be arrested/killed) = -20 CP.

### **Secret Identity** [-10 CP]
Must protect civilian identity. Enemies would target family/friends if discovered. Must balance hero work with civilian life.

### **Sense of Duty** [-2 to -15 CP]
Feel obligated to protect specific group. Friends = -5 CP, Nation = -10 CP, Humanity = -15 CP. Will not abandon them even at personal risk.

### **Social Stigma** [-5 to -20 CP]
Society treats you poorly due to background. Minor (foreigner) = -5 CP, Serious (ex-con, mutant) = -10 CP, Extreme (monster, undead) = -20 CP.

### **Vow** [-5 to -15 CP]
Sworn oath you cannot break. Minor (vegetarian, pacifism) = -5 CP, Serious (poverty, chastity) = -10 CP, Major (protect specific person at all costs) = -15 CP.

## SUPERNATURAL DISADVANTAGES

### **Dependency** [-10 to -25 CP]
Require specific substance to survive. Without it, lose 1 HP/hour. Rare substance (moonlight, specific crystal) = -10 CP, Very rare (phoenix tears) = -25 CP.

### **Dread** [-10 CP]
Specific substance/situation causes weakness. Take extra 1d6 damage from source. Examples: silver, holy symbols, sunlight.

### **Weakness (Element)** [-15 CP]
Take ×1.5 damage from specific element (fire, cold, electricity, etc.).

### **Cursed** [-10 to -20 CP]
Under magical curse. Minor (bad luck, -1 to all rolls 1 day/month) = -10 CP, Major (transform monthly, hunted by supernatural being) = -20 CP.

### **Vulnerability** [-10 CP]
Specific attack type ignores your resistances/immunities. Example: Silver bullets bypass Physical Resistance.

### **Distinctive Features** [-5 CP]
Obvious unusual appearance (glowing eyes, blue skin, horns). Disadvantage on Stealth in public, can't disguise.

### **Haunted** [-10 CP]
Followed by ghost/spirit. Appears at inopportune times, distracts you. GM decides when.

### **Unlucky** [-10 CP]
Once per session, GM can force you to reroll successful check or save (must use new result).

## POWER-RELATED DISADVANTAGES

### **Berserker** [-10 CP]
When using rage/power-up ability, must attack nearest target (ally or enemy) each round. Can't use defensive abilities or retreat while raging.

### **Blatant Power** [-10 CP]
Your powers are obvious and flashy. Can't use powers subtly. Everyone within 100 ft knows when you use abilities.

### **Costs Fatigue** [-10 CP per power]
Using specific power costs 1 FP in addition to normal costs. Applies to one advantage purchased.

### **Limited Uses** [-5 CP per power]
Specific power can only be used 3 times per long rest (instead of normal uses). Applies to one advantage.

### **Power Loss** [-10 to -25 CP]
Powers fail under specific circumstance. Uncommon (wet) = -10 CP, Common (stressed, 50% of time) = -25 CP.

### **Uncontrollable Power** [-15 CP]
One power activates randomly when stressed. Make WIS save DC 13 when taking damage or power activates uncontrollably.

### **Fragile** [-10 CP]
Take double damage from area effects (Fireball, explosions, etc.).

### **Slow Healing** [-10 CP]
Natural healing takes twice as long. Magical healing works normally.

**Difficulty**: GM sets DC (5 = trivial, 30 = nearly impossible)

# COMBAT

## Combat Sequence

**1. Determine Surprise**: Surprised creatures can't act in first round  
**2. Roll Initiative**: 1d20 + DEX modifier (+ bonuses)  
**3. Take Turns**: Highest initiative to lowest  
**4. Repeat**: Start new round at step 3

---

## Your Turn

On your turn, you can:
- **Move** up to your speed
- **Take one Action**
- **Take one Bonus Action** (if you have one)
- **Use one Reaction** (once per round, on any turn)
- **Free Actions**: Talk (briefly), drop item, open door

### Actions

**Attack**: Make one weapon attack (or more with Extra Attack)  
**Cast a Spell**: Cast one spell (action casting time)  
**Dash**: Move again (double movement this turn)  
**Disengage**: Move without provoking opportunity attacks  
**Dodge**: Attacks against you have disadvantage until your next turn  
**Help**: Give ally advantage on next ability check or attack  
**Hide**: Make Stealth check to hide  
**Ready**: Prepare action triggered by condition you specify  
**Search**: Make Perception or Investigation check  
**Use Object**: Interact with object (open door, pull lever)

### Bonus Actions

**Class Features**: Many class features use bonus action  
**Two-Weapon Fighting**: Attack with off-hand weapon  
**Cunning Action**: Trickster/Martial Artist abilities

### Reactions

**Opportunity Attack**: When enemy leaves your reach, make melee attack  
**Active Defense**: Add ability + proficiency + enhanced to AC vs one attack  
**Readied Action**: Use prepared action when trigger occurs  
**Class Features**: Many class features use reaction

---

## Movement

**Speed**: Most heroes move 30 ft/round  
**Difficult Terrain**: Costs 2 ft of movement per 1 ft moved (rubble, stairs, crowds)  
**Climbing/Swimming**: Costs 2 ft of movement per 1 ft climbed  
**Standing from Prone**: Costs half your movement  
**Jumping**: Long jump = STR ft (with 10 ft run), High jump = 3 + STR modifier ft

---

## Attacks

### Making an Attack

**1. Choose Target**: Within range  
**2. Determine Modifiers**: Advantage/disadvantage, cover, etc.  
**3. Roll Attack**: 1d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus (if proficient)  
**4. Compare to AC**: Hit if attack roll ≥ target's AC  
**5. Roll Damage**: Roll weapon damage dice + ability modifier

### Attack Rolls

**Melee Attack**: 1d20 + STR modifier + proficiency (if proficient)  
**Ranged Attack**: 1d20 + DEX modifier + proficiency (if proficient)  
**Finesse Weapon**: Choose STR or DEX

**Natural 20**: Critical hit (roll damage dice twice, add modifiers once)  
**Natural 1**: Automatic miss (no other effect in v3.0)

### Damage

**Roll Damage Dice**: Listed on weapon  
**Add Ability Modifier**: STR for melee, DEX for ranged (finesse = your choice)  
**Apply Resistance/Vulnerability**: Half damage (resistance) or double (vulnerability)

---

## Cover

**Half Cover** (+2 AC): Low wall, furniture, creatures  
**Three-Quarters Cover** (+5 AC): Portcullis, narrow opening  
**Total Cover** (can't be targeted): Completely behind obstacle

---

## Special Combat Rules

### Flanking (Optional)
When you and ally are on opposite sides of enemy, both have advantage on melee attacks against that enemy.

### Grappling
**Action**: Contested STR (Athletics) vs. target's STR (Athletics) or DEX (Acrobatics)  
**Success**: Target is grappled (speed 0, disadvantage on attacks vs. others)  
**Escape**: Target uses action to repeat contest

### Shoving
**Action**: Contested STR (Athletics) vs. target's STR (Athletics) or DEX (Acrobatics)  
**Success**: Push target 5 ft or knock prone

### Two-Weapon Fighting
**When**: Attack with light weapon in each hand  
**Bonus Action**: Make attack with off-hand weapon  
**Damage**: Don't add ability modifier to damage (unless you have Two-Weapon Fighting style)

### Ranged Attacks in Melee
**Disadvantage**: Ranged attacks have disadvantage if enemy within 5 ft (unless Crossbow Expert or similar)

---

## Heroic Recovery (Universal)

**All characters have access to Heroic Recovery**:
- **Bonus action, 1/short rest**
- Roll one Hit Die + (CON modifier × 2)
- Regain that much HP
- Represents catching breath, adrenaline surge, gritting through pain

**Example**: Paragon (d12 HD, CON +3) rolls 1d12 + 6, regains 7-18 HP

---

## Active Defense (Universal)

**All characters can use Active Defense**:
- **Reaction when hit**
- Add ability modifier + proficiency bonus + Enhanced Active Defense (if purchased) to AC vs that one attack
- If total AC now exceeds attack roll, the attack misses
- Can only use if you can see attacker

**Example**: DEX +3, Prof +3, Enhanced +2 = add +8 AC vs one attack

---

## Conditions

**Blinded**: Can't see, attacks have disadvantage, attacks against you have advantage  
**Charmed**: Can't attack charmer, charmer has advantage on social checks  
**Frightened**: Disadvantage on checks and attacks while source of fear visible, can't willingly move toward source  
**Grappled**: Speed 0, can't benefit from speed bonuses  
**Incapacitated**: Can't take actions or reactions  
**Invisible**: Impossible to see without special sense, attacks have advantage, attacks against have disadvantage  
**Paralyzed**: Incapacitated, can't move/speak, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage, crits if within 5 ft  
**Poisoned**: Disadvantage on attacks and ability checks  
**Prone**: Disadvantage on attacks, attacks against you have advantage (if within 5 ft), standing costs half movement  
**Restrained**: Speed 0, attacks have disadvantage, attacks against you have advantage, disadvantage on DEX saves  
**Stunned**: Incapacitated, can't move, can speak falteringly, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage  
**Unconscious**: Incapacitated, can't move/speak, unaware, drop held items, fall prone, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage, crits if within 5 ft

---

## Damage & Healing

### Hit Points
**Current HP**: Your health now  
**Maximum HP**: Determined by class Hit Die + (CON × 2) per level  
**0 HP**: You fall unconscious and are dying

### Damage Types
**Physical**: Bludgeoning, Slashing, Piercing  
**Energy**: Fire, Cold, Lightning, Acid, Radiant, Necrotic  
**Special**: Force, Poison, Psychic

### Dying
**At 0 HP**:
- Fall unconscious
- Can't take actions/reactions
- Make death saving throws

**Death Saving Throws**:
- Start of your turn (if 0 HP), roll d20
- **10+**: Success (mark one success)
- **9 or less**: Failure (mark one failure)
- **Natural 20**: Regain 1 HP (stand up!)
- **Natural 1**: Counts as two failures
- **3 Successes**: Stabilize (unconscious but not dying)
- **3 Failures**: You die

**Taking Damage at 0 HP**:
- One failed death save
- Critical hit = two failed death saves

**Massive Damage**:
- If damage reduces you to negative HP ≥ your max HP, you die instantly
- Example: 50 max HP, take 100 damage = instant death

### Stabilizing
**Medicine Check DC 10**: Action, stabilize dying creature  
**Healing**: Any healing brings creature to positive HP and conscious

---

## Rest & Recovery

### Short Rest
**Duration**: 1 hour  
**Benefits**:
- Spend Hit Dice to heal (roll 1d[HD] + CON×2 per die spent)
- Some class features recharge
- Heroic Recovery recharges

### Long Rest
**Duration**: 8 hours (at least 6 sleeping)  
**Benefits**:
- Regain all HP
- Regain half your Hit Dice (minimum 1)
- All class features recharge
- Remove 1 level of exhaustion

# ADVANCEMENT

## Leveling Up

When you gain enough XP (or GM says you level up):

**1. Increase HP**: Roll your Hit Die (or take average) + (CON × 2)  
**2. Gain Class Features**: Check your class table  
**3. Gain 10 CP**: Spend on new advantages or equipment  
**4. Ability Score Increase** (at certain levels): +2 to one ability or +1 to two  
**5. Update Proficiency**: Increases at levels 5, 9, 13, 17  

### Proficiency Bonus by Level

| Level | Prof Bonus |
|-------|-----------|
| 1-4 | +2 |
| 5-8 | +3 |
| 9-12 | +4 |
| 13-16 | +5 |
| 17-20 | +6 |

## Multiclassing

### Requirements
Must have minimum 13 in multiclass ability score:

| Class | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| Paragon | STR 13 and CHA 13 |
| Juggernaut | STR 13 and CON 13 |
| Gadgeteer | INT 13 |
| Mystic | INT 13 or WIS 13 |
| Martial Artist | DEX 13 or STR 13 |
| Speedster | DEX 13 |
| Metamorph | CON 13 |
| Mentalist | WIS 13 or CHA 13 |
| Conduit | CON 13 and WIS 13 |
| Trickster | DEX 13 and CHA 13 |

### What You Gain

**When you take first level in new class**:
- Hit points from new class's Hit Die
- New class's features at that level
- New weapon/armor proficiencies (limited, see table)
- Access to that class's locked abilities

**What You Don't Gain**:
- All proficiencies (only some, see table)
- Starting equipment (only Level 1 class gives this)

### Multiclass Example

**Level 1-3**: Martial Artist (Weapon Master)  
**Level 4**: Take 1 level Speedster (now Martial Artist 3/Speedster 1)

**Gains**: Enhanced Move 1, Burst of Speed, Ki Points (if didn't have), access to Speedster-locked abilities  
**Doesn't Gain**: All Speedster weapon proficiencies (already have from Martial Artist)

# CAMPAIGN GUIDELINES

## Session Structure

**Typical 4-Hour Session**:
- 30 min: Recap, planning, roleplay
- 60 min: Investigation, social encounters
- 30 min: Complication, twist
- 60 min: Combat encounter
- 30 min: Resolution, next hook

## Types of Adventures

**Mystery**: Investigate crime, find clues, confront villain  
**Rescue**: Save hostages, stop execution, extract target  
**Defense**: Protect location/person from attack  
**Heist**: Infiltrate, steal objective, escape  
**Chase**: Pursue or evade, across city/time/dimensions  
**War**: Large-scale conflict, command troops, battlefield tactics

## Villains

**Street-Level**: Crime bosses, gang leaders, serial killers, corrupt officials  
**Superheroic**: Supervillains, evil corporations, alien invaders, mad scientists  
**Cosmic**: World-eaters, reality warpers, demon lords, rogue gods

**Good Villain Traits**:
- Clear motivation (not "evil for evil's sake")
- Personal connection to heroes
- Challenges heroes' beliefs
- Has minions/organization
- Escalating threat
