## "SHADOWSTRIKE" - Street-Level Vigilante

---

## CHARACTER CONCEPT

**Name**: Marcus Kane / "Shadowstrike"  
**Archetype**: Batman meets Daredevil - stealth-based vigilante who uses fear and precision strikes  
**Power Level**: Street-Level Superheroic (Level 5, 200 CP)  
**Class**: Martial Artist (Tactical Fighter subclass)  
**Background**: Former special forces operator turned vigilante after family killed by organized crime

**Core Fantasy**: 
- Strike from shadows, disappear before enemies react
- Use fear and intimidation as weapons
- Master tactician who plans every move
- Expert with escrima sticks and thrown weapons
- No superpowers, just peak human ability and training

## STEP-BY-STEP CHARACTER CREATION

### STEP 1: Choose Power Level & Class

**Power Level**: Street-Level Superheroic  
- Starting at Level 5
- **Character Points Available**: 150 (street level base) + 50 (5 levels × 10 CP/level) = **200 CP**

### STEP 3: Advantages (Custom Build)

**Combat Advantages**:

1. **Combat Reflexes** [15 CP]
   - +1 to initiative
   - Never surprised
   - **Why**: Strike first, control engagement

2. **Weapon Master (Escrima Sticks)** [20 CP]
   - Proficiency + expertise with escrima sticks
   - Can use DEX for attack/damage (finesse)
   - Critical hit on 19-20
   - **Why**: Signature weapons, reliable damage

3. **Enhanced Active Defense +2** [20 CP]
   - Add +2 to Dodge/Parry/Block reactions (in addition to ability modifier + proficiency)
   - **Why**: Survive longer in melee range

**Stealth & Mobility**:

4. **Shadowy** [15 CP]
   - Advantage on Stealth checks in dim light or darkness
   - Can hide when only lightly obscured
   - **Why**: Core to vigilante concept

5. **Catfall** [10 CP]
   - Reduce fall damage by 10 × level (50 ft at Level 5)
   - Always land on feet
   - **Why**: Rooftop movement

6. **Night Vision** [10 CP]
   - See in dim light as if bright light
   - See in darkness as if dim light (60 ft)
   - **Why**: Operate at night without light sources

**Tactical Advantages**:

7. **Danger Sense** [15 CP]
   - Can't be surprised while conscious
   - Advantage on initiative rolls
   - **Why**: Always prepared, stacks with Combat Reflexes

8. **Eidetic Memory** [10 CP]
   - Perfect recall of everything seen/heard
   - Advantage on INT checks to remember information
   - **Why**: Batman-style detective work

**Defensive Advantage**:

9. **Tough as Nails 2** [30 CP]
   - Heal +2 HP per Hit Die spent (Heroic Recovery, short rests)
   - **Why**: Limited healing at street level, need sustain

**Total Advantages**: 145 CP

**CP Spent So Far**: 175 CP (25 remaining)

**Disadvantages**:

1. **Code of Honor (Hero's)** [-15 CP]

   - Will protect innocents even at personal risk
   - Will honor promises
   - **Roleplay**: Marcus has strict rules, Batman-style

2. **Obsession (Destroy Organized Crime)** [-10 CP]
   - Must pursue leads on crime families

3. **Secret Identity** [-10 CP]
   - Must protect civilian identity

4. **Loner** [-5 CP]

**Total Disadvantages**: -40 CP (gain 40 CP back)

### COMBAT STATISTICS

**Hit Points**: 52 HP
- Calculation: (10+4) + (6+4) + (7+4) + (5+4) + (6+4) = 52
- Formula: d10 + (CON modifier × 2) per level

**Armor Class**: 16
- Calculation: 10 (base) + 3 (DEX) + 3 (armor) = 16
- **With Active Defense (Dodge)**: 16 + 3 (DEX) + 3 (Prof) + 2 (Enhanced) = **24 AC** (vs one attack, reaction)

**Initiative**: +7

### SPECIAL FEATURES

**Heroic Recovery** (Universal Feature):
- Bonus action, 1/short rest
- Roll 1d10 + 4 (CON × 2) + 2 (Tough as Nails 2) = **1d10+6 HP**
- Average heal: 11 HP

**Active Defense** (Reaction):
- Choose Dodge, Parry, or Block as reaction
- Add: DEX (+3) + Prof (+3) + Enhanced (+2) = **+8 AC** vs one attack
- Example: AC 16 becomes AC 24 against one attack

**Stealth Expertise**:
- In darkness: Stealth +9 with advantage
- Effectively: Roll 2d20+9, take higher
- Average roll: ~23 (very hard to spot)

## COMBAT TACTICS

### ENGAGEMENT SEQUENCE

**Phase 1: Reconnaissance** (Before Combat)
1. Use Eidetic Memory + Investigation to study patrol patterns
2. Use Night Vision + Stealth to get into position
3. Use Perception to identify priority targets (leaders, armed)
4. Plan escape route (Grappling Hook positioning)

**Phase 2: Alpha Strike** (Round 1)
1. **Surprise Attack** from shadows (enemies can't be surprised due to Danger Sense, but you get surprise)
2. **Action**: Attack priority target with Precision Attack maneuver
   - Roll: 1d20+9 (attack) + 1d8 (Precision if needed)
   - Damage: 1d6+5 (main) + 1d6+3 (off-hand)
3. **Bonus Action**: Third attack (Two-Weapon Fighting)
   - Attack: 1d20+9
   - Damage: 1d6+3
4. **Total Damage Round 1**: ~20-30 damage (likely down one enemy)

**Phase 3: Sustained Combat** (Rounds 2-4)
1. **High Initiative** (+7) means you usually go first
2. **Positioning**: Use Step of the Wind (Ki Point) to Disengage + reposition if surrounded
3. **Attacks**: Focus fire priority targets
4. **Maneuvers**:
   - Use **Disarming Strike** on enemies with guns (remove ranged threat)
   - Use **Riposte** when enemies miss (punish mistakes)
   - Use **Precision Attack** on critical hits (guarantee hit)
5. **Defense**: Use Active Defense reaction if taking big hit (AC 16 → 24)

**Phase 4: Exit Strategy** (When HP < 50%)
1. Use **Smoke Bomb** (action, create heavily obscured area)
2. Use **Step of the Wind** (Ki Point, bonus action, Disengage)
3. Use **Grappling Hook** (move action, climb to rooftop)
4. Use **Shadowy + Stealth** (hide in darkness with advantage, +9)
5. Escape or reposition for another strike

### SAMPLE COMBAT ROUND

**Shadowstrike vs 3 Gang Members**

**Initiative**: Shadowstrike rolls 1d20+7 = 24 (goes first)

**Shadowstrike's Turn**:
- **Action**: Attack Gang Member 1 (leader with gun)
  - Main Attack: Rolls 16+9 = 25, **HIT!** Damage: 1d6+5 = 9 bludgeoning
  - Disarming Strike: Adds 1d8 = 6 damage, total **15 damage**, STR save DC 14 or drop gun
  - Gang Member: STR save rolls 8, **FAILS**, drops gun
  - Extra Attack: Rolls 18+9 = 27, **HIT!** Damage: 1d6+3 = 7 bludgeoning
  - Total to Gang Member 1: **22 damage** (likely down)
  
- **Bonus Action**: Attack Gang Member 2
  - Off-hand Attack: Rolls 14+9 = 23, **HIT!** Damage: 1d6+3 = 8 bludgeoning

- **Movement**: 5 ft step to avoid being flanked

**Enemy Turn 1** (Gang Member 2):
- Attacks Shadowstrike: Rolls 13+3 = 16 vs AC 16, **HIT!** Damage: 1d6+1 = 5 bludgeoning
- Shadowstrike HP: 52 → 47

**Enemy Turn 2** (Gang Member 3):
- Attacks Shadowstrike: Rolls 11+3 = 14 vs AC 16, **MISS**
- Shadowstrike uses **Riposte** (reaction, spend Superiority Die)
  - Attack: Rolls 15+9 = 24, **HIT!** Damage: 1d6+3+1d8 = 13 bludgeoning
  - Gang Member 3 HP: 15 → 2

**Round 1 Result**:
- Gang Member 1: DOWN (22 damage)
- Gang Member 2: 15 → 7 HP (bloodied)
- Gang Member 3: 15 → 2 HP (nearly down)
- Shadowstrike: 52 → 47 HP (90%)

**Round 2**: Shadowstrike finishes remaining enemies, takes 1 more hit (47 → 42 HP), combat ends

**Post-Combat**: 
- Use Heroic Recovery (bonus action): 1d10+6 = 12 HP
- HP: 42 → 52 (full HP recovered!)
- Short rest to regain Ki Points and Superiority Dice
