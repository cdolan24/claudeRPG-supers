## DERIVED STATISTICS

### Hit Points (HP)
**Formula**: Hit Die + CON modifier per level
- **Level 1**: Maximum HD + CON modifier
- **Higher Levels**: Roll HD + CON modifier (or take average)

### Fatigue Points (FP)
**Formula**: 10 + CON modifier + Level
- Used to power abilities, advantages, and sustained efforts
- Recover 1/2 FP on short rest, all FP on long rest

### Basic Move
**Formula**: 30 feet + (DEX modifier √ó 5 feet)
- Your base movement speed per round (6 seconds)
- Enhanced Move advantage increases this dramatically

### Initiative
**Formula**: DEX modifier + any bonuses
- Roll d20 + Initiative when combat starts

## DEFENSE MECHANICS

### Innate Armor Class (AC)
**Formula**: 10 + DEX modifier + DR bonus
- **DR Bonus**: (Total Damage Resistance √∑ 5), round down
- Example: DR 15 = +3 AC bonus
- Armor and shields can increase AC further

### Psionic Armor Class
**Formula**: 10 + WIS modifier + Mind Shield bonus
- Only used against mental attacks (telepathy, mind control, etc.)
- Mind Shield advantage increases this

### Active Defense (Reaction)
When attacked, you can use your reaction to actively defend, adding a bonus to your AC **for that specific attack only**:

**Dodge** (DEX-based): +2 to AC vs one attack you can see
- Requires: Mobility, awareness of attack
- Enhanced Dodge advantage increases bonus

**Parry** (STR-based): +2 to AC vs one melee attack you can see
- Requires: Melee weapon or unarmed combat readiness
- Enhanced Parry advantage increases bonus

**Block** (CON-based): +3 to AC vs one attack you can see
- Requires: Shield or similar defensive item
- Enhanced Block advantage increases bonus

**Mind Shield** (WIS-based): +2 to Psionic AC vs one mental attack
- Requires: Mind Shield advantage
- Protects against telepathy, domination, fear

## OFFENSE MECHANICS

### Basic Strike (Physical)
**Formula**: ((STR + DEX) √∑ 4) + Proficiency Bonus (if proficient in unarmed combat)
- Your baseline unarmed attack modifier
- Roll: d20 + Basic Strike
- Damage: 1 + STR modifier

### Mental Kick (Psionic)
**Formula**: ((INT + WIS) √∑ 6) + Proficiency Bonus (if proficient in mental attacks)
- Your baseline mental attack modifier
- Used for psychic blasts, telekinetic shoves, etc.
- Roll: d20 + Mental Kick
- Damage: 1d6 + INT or WIS modifier (your choice)

### Weapon Attacks
**Formula**: d20 + Proficiency Bonus + ability modifier + weapon bonus
- **Melee**: Usually STR modifier
- **Ranged**: Usually DEX modifier
- **Finesse**: Choose STR or DEX
- **Damage**: Weapon dice + ability modifier

### Innate Attacks (Powers)
**Formula**: d20 + Proficiency Bonus + ability modifier + Specialized Skill bonus
- Fire blast, ice beam, energy projection, etc.
- Damage: Based on advantage level purchased
- Can be improved with Specialized Skills

## SPECIALIZED SKILLS

Specialized Skills are trained proficiencies that add bonuses to specific actions.

### Combat Specialized Skills
- **Innate Attack (Type)**: +1 to hit per rank with specific innate attack
- **Martial Arts Style**: +1 to hit per rank with unarmed/style weapons
- **Weapon Group (Type)**: +1 to hit per rank with weapon category

### Movement Specialized Skills
- **Running**: Add bonus to Vigilance checks for long-distance running
- **Climbing**: Add bonus to Athletics checks for climbing
- **Flying**: Add bonus to Acrobatics checks while airborne
- **Swimming**: Add bonus to Athletics checks in water

### Technical Specialized Skills
- **Hacking**: Add bonus to Machinery checks for computer systems
- **Engineering**: Add bonus to Machinery checks for building/repairing
- **Battlesuit**: Required for exosuit use, adds bonus to all checks while wearing

### Esoteric Specialized Skills
- **Spellcasting**: Add bonus to spell attack rolls and spell save DCs
- **Psionics**: Add bonus to psionic attack rolls
- **Meditation**: Add bonus to recovering FP during short rests

**Cost**: 2 character points per +1 bonus (max +5)
